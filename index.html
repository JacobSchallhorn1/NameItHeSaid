What is a meme?

The age old question. If you are past your teenage years and aren't a frequent user of social media or the Internet in general, then you have most likely asked this very question. Wikipedia will tell you this. However if you didn't understand that then let me dumb it down a little. A meme is an inside joke that is usually in the form of a picture or video. People find them funny because they can relate to them or understand the "secret" meaning behind them.However there are outlier memes that don't fit this description but I will get to those later. Memes usually have many different layers to them such as their content,template, and merging of multiple of each.

To the right you will see an example of a meme. The joke with this image is that Trump has build a persona on the internet that he wants to build a wall. He wants to keep from having immigrants enter the country illegally. However the internet took this serious topic and turned it into a meme. The funny part about this is that trump wants to build a wall so it fits perfectly into this situation. Also with this meme you can see an example of a meme format/template. Memes often use the same template in order to easily organize the meme and/or reference the original.
