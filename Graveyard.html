The Meme Reaper

When a meme has past it's prime the Ender of memes comes to collect it and add it to The Meme Graveyard. The man on the right is PewDiePie. He is the most subscribed youtuber and puts out content about memes. One series that he does is *Clap,Clap* meme review. Once a meme gets reviewed on meme review it only has a limited time left on this Earth. In fact meme review has even become its own meme in a way. Few memes have ever escaped the black hole of eternal staleness that is meme review.

*Clap,Clap*




Before Every episode of meme review starts PewDiePie Says "Meme Review" and claps to meme and review. This has become an iconic meme among the community.
